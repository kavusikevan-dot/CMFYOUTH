Our Vision

Our vision is to provide education to children in the underprivileged areas in Iran. No matter their economic, geographic, and additional setbacks, all children deserve the right to education.

We aim to create an environment that nurtures creativity, offers equal opportunities for all, and fosters valuable education. Through this, we shape a brighter and more creative society and help create a better future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower children in Iran by providing access to equal education, technology, and books, promoting a deeper love of learning, and creating opportunities for a brighter future.

We believe every child deserves the chance to learn, grow, and reach their full potential regardless of their circumstances.
